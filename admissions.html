Admissions 2024-25

Begin your engineering journey at WCE Sangli. We welcome talented students ready to embrace challenges, innovate, and transform the future through technology.

Programs Offered

🎓

B.E. (Bachelor of Engineering)

4-year full-time undergraduate program offering specializations in 6 engineering disciplines with AICTE approval and NBA accreditation.

📚

M.Tech (Master of Technology)

2-year postgraduate program focusing on advanced research and specialization in various engineering fields for engineering graduates.

🔬

Ph.D. Programs

Doctoral research programs in collaboration with renowned universities for advanced scientific research and innovation in engineering.

Admission Process

📝

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the academic and eligibility requirements for your chosen program before applying.

📋

Step 2: Online Application

Complete the online application form with your personal, academic, and contact information before the deadline.

✅

Step 3: Document Verification

Submit original documents including certificates, mark sheets, and entrance exam scores for verification.

🎯

Step 4: Merit Selection

Candidates are selected based on merit as per the respective state counseling process or direct admission.

📞

Step 5: Confirmation

Confirm your admission by paying the required fees and completing registration formalities.

🎉

Step 6: Orientation

Participate in campus orientation program to familiarize yourself with campus life and academic culture.

Eligibility Criteria

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.)

✓
Passed 12th (HSC/Equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from recognized board
✓
Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (45% for reserved categories)
✓
Valid JEE Main/State CET score within the prescribed cutoff
✓
Indian citizen or NRI/PIO as per AICTE regulations
✓
Completed 17 years of age at the time of admission
