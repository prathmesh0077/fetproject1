Admissions 2025–26

Join WCE Sangli through JoSAA, CCMT, CCMN, and CAP processes. Transparent, merit-based admissions for B.E., M.E., MBA, MCA, and Ph.D. programmes.

Admission Routes
🎓
B.E. / B.Tech (UG)
Admissions through JoSAA (NIT quota) and CAP (State quota) based on JEE Main and MHT-CET scores. 15% seats reserved for All India quota via JoSAA.
📘
M.E. / M.Tech (PG)
Postgraduate admissions via CCMT (NIT quota based on GATE score) and state-level CAP based on PGCET. 15% seats filled through CCMT All India quota.
🔬
Ph.D. Programme
Doctoral admissions twice a year (July & January). Eligibility based on GATE/NET scores or department entrance test. Full-time, part-time, and sponsored categories available.
Admission Process – Step by Step
1
Appear for Entrance Exam
JEE Main for B.E., GATE for M.E., PGCET for PG programmes, MHT-CET for state quota seats.
2
Register on Portal
Register on JoSAA / CCMT / CAP portal with valid credentials, exam scores and documents.
3
Fill Choice Form
Fill college and branch preferences in order. WCE Sangli is listed as NIT-equivalent under JoSAA and CCMT.
4
Seat Allotment
Multiple rounds of counselling take place. Check allotment result and accept / reject / upgrade seat as per rules.
5
Document Verification
Report to WCE Sangli with original documents. Complete physical verification and fee payment at the institute.
6
Enrolment & Induction
Receive admission confirmation, college ID, and attend the mandatory induction / orientation programme.
