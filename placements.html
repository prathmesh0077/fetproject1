Placement Process
1
Pre-Placement TrainingAll final-year students undergo aptitude, coding, communication, and GD/PI training sessions conducted by the Training & Placement Cell throughout Semester VII.
2
Company Registration & Job PostingCompanies register with the T&P Cell, share JDs, and schedule campus drives. WCE's T&P portal lists all open opportunities for eligible students.
3
Aptitude & Online TestsShortlisting rounds include written aptitude tests, online coding challenges, or domain-specific assessments conducted on campus or via proctored platforms.
4
Technical InterviewsOne or more rounds of technical interviews assessing core subject knowledge, problem-solving, and project experience. Conducted by company engineers and HRs.
5
HR Interview & Offer LetterFinal HR round assessing communication, cultural fit, and expectations. Selected candidates receive formal offer letters on the same day or within a week.
