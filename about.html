College Introduction

Walchand College of Engineering (WCE), Sangli is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions in Maharashtra, India. Established in 1947, it is a Government-Aided Autonomous Institute affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur. The college has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in India and holds NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation with NBA-accredited programmes.

The institute spans a lush campus of over 40 acres in the heart of Sangli city. WCE offers undergraduate (B.E./B.Tech), postgraduate (M.E./M.Tech/M.B.A.), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programmes across 15 engineering and technology departments. With over 4,766 enrolled students and 316 faculty members, the college maintains a strong commitment to academic rigour, research innovation, and holistic student development.

WCE has produced generations of distinguished alumni who lead globally across industries, startups, research institutions, and public service. The institution's robust placement record, research output, and state-of-the-art infrastructure make it a destination of choice for aspiring engineers across the country.

Our History
1947
Foundation Walchand College of Engineering was founded by the visionary industrialist Seth Walchand Hirachand Doshi, with an inaugural batch in Civil Engineering. The college was established to meet the growing engineering education needs of independent India.
1960s
Expansion & Growth New departments including Mechanical, Electrical, and Electronics Engineering were introduced, broadening the scope of technical education at WCE and responding to industrial demand.
1980s
Postgraduate & Research Programmes WCE launched M.E./M.Tech postgraduate programmes and established a dedicated research culture. Ph.D. programmes were introduced, laying the groundwork for a strong research ecosystem.
2000s
Autonomous Status & Accreditation The college received Autonomous Institute status from UGC and Shivaji University. NAAC accreditation and NBA approvals were obtained, significantly enhancing academic quality and global recognition.
2010s
Research & Innovation Drive WCE established multiple research centres, innovation labs, and industry partnerships. Publication output surpassed 8,000+ with 275+ patents, and placement packages reached new highs with top global recruiters.
2025
NAAC 'A' Grade Reaccreditation WCE successfully retained its NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation for 2025–30, reaffirming its commitment to quality education, research excellence, and student outcomes. Multiple programmes remain NBA accredited.
