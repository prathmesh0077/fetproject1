Research Laboratories
🤖
AI & Machine Learning Lab
Computer Science & Engineering
Dedicated to deep learning, computer vision, NLP, and intelligent systems. Houses GPU clusters and datasets for large-scale AI experiments. Active collaborations with IITB and industry partners.
⚡
Power Electronics & Drives Lab
Electrical Engineering
Research on EV drives, power converters, renewable energy integration, and smart grid systems. Funded by DST and MNRE for sustainable energy projects.
🔬
Nanotechnology & Materials Lab
Applied Chemistry / Chemical Engineering
Synthesizes and characterizes nanomaterials for energy, biomedical, and environmental applications. Equipped with SEM, XRD, FTIR, and UV-Vis spectroscopy instruments.
🏗️
Structural & Geotechnical Lab
Civil Engineering
Research in advanced structural materials, seismic analysis, foundation engineering, and smart construction. Supports state and national infrastructure projects.
🛰️
VLSI & Embedded Systems Lab
Electronics & Telecommunication
FPGA-based prototyping, SoC design, and IoT system development. Students work on real-world embedded solutions in collaboration with semiconductor companies.
🌱
Environmental Engineering Lab
Civil Engineering
Water quality analysis, wastewater treatment, air pollution monitoring, and sustainability research. Government-funded projects for clean drinking water initiatives in rural Maharashtra.
